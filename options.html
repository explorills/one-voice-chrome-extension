ONE voice (ChatGPT)

Default 600 (10 min). Recording auto-stops at this limit.
When on: transcription is copied to clipboard AND submitted to the current ChatGPT conversation (so your custom grammar-check GPT processes it). When off: copy only.
Pauses media playing in YouTube, YouTube Music, and Spotify web tabs while recording; resumes automatically when the transcription is captured. First enable will prompt for permission on those two sites only.
Hotkey: bind at chrome://extensions/shortcuts (set scope to Global).
